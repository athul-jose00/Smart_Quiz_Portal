Core Features

Our platform addresses the limitations of traditional assessment methods

Automated Grading

Instant scoring eliminates manual grading, providing immediate results to students and teachers.

Role-based System

Distinct interfaces for Admins, Teachers, and Students with appropriate permissions.

Class Management

Teachers create classes with unique codes that students use to join multiple classes.

Performance Analytics

Track student progress over time with detailed reports and visualizations.

Secure Platform

Encrypted passwords and session management ensure data security and privacy.

Timed Assessments

Set time limits for quizzes to simulate exam conditions and manage class time effectively.
